🚨 Production Release Schedule & Constraints

📅 Allowed Release Days

  • Tuesday - Production releases
  • Thursday - Production releases
  • No releases on Mon, Wed, Fri
  • No weekend releases

🚫 Release Freeze Periods

  • Month-End: Last 3 business days
  • Year-End: Dec 15 - Jan 10
  • Emergency fixes need executive approval
  • Plan sprints around freeze periods
⏰ Important Timeline: RFC must be submitted 48 hours before release day. Pre-prod validation should complete by Monday (for Tuesday) or Wednesday (for Thursday).
